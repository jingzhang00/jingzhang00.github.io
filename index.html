Jing Zhang

I’m currently a second-year master student’s at the Chalmers University of Technology, majoring in System, Control and Mechatronics, under the supervision of Prof. Karinne Ramirez-Amaro. Before that, I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Microelectronics Science and Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.


My research interests focuses on developing advanced robotic systems for complex, human-centric interactions and tasks. This encompasses enhancing the cognitive abilities of robots to better understand and adapt not only to social environments but also to intricate task-specific scenarios. I aim to realize more natural and efficient interactions for human-robot collaboration.
